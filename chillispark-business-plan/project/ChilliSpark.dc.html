About ChilliSpark

We help local
businesses win online.

ChilliSpark is a Delhi-based web design and development studio. We build fast, modern, affordable websites that give small businesses a professional presence they own — not just another social profile.

Our story

We started ChilliSpark because too many great local businesses — dentists, clinics, restaurants, salons — were losing customers to competitors with better websites, or to marketplaces that own the relationship.

Our job isn't to sell you a website. It's to help you get more customers, build trust, and control your own brand — with a site that loads fast, works perfectly on mobile, and turns visitors into inquiries.
